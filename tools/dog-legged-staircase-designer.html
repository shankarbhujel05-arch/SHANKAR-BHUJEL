Formulas (NBC 205):
Net Rise = Storey Height − Slab Thickness
No. Risers = Round(Net Rise ÷ R) → even number (symmetric)
Actual R = Net Rise ÷ No. Risers
2R + T = 600–640 mm (comfort rule)
Risers/flight = Total Risers ÷ 2
Treads/flight = Risers/flight − 1
Going = Treads/flight × T
Well Width = 2×FlightW + MidWall
Well Length = Going + Landing Width
Pitch = atan(R ÷ T) × 180/π
1 foot = 304.8 mm · 1 inch = 25.4 mm
ℹ️ NBC 205: Residential — R ≤ 175 mm (6′′⅞), T ≥ 250 mm (9′′⅞), W ≥ 1000 mm (3′-3″), HR ≥ 2000 mm (6′-6½″).
Public — R ≤ 150 mm (5′′⅞), T ≥ 270 mm (10′′⅝), W ≥ 1500 mm (4′-11″), HR ≥ 2200 mm (7′-2½″).
Comfort rule 2R+T = 600–640 mm (23⅝″–25¼″) applies to all occupancies.
